    • Research

    My research primarily focuses on how to develop Trusted Artificial Intelligence (AI). AI is increasingly being embedded into the fabric of society as a decision making tool; as such, ensuring that it aligns to our values, it is reliable and safe is pertinent. I develop algorithms to ensure robustness of deep learning models for example, being able to detect when such models are processing adversarial input. I also work on techniques for explainability/fairness of machine learning models for example, being able to characterise subgroups of a population that may be adversely affected by a predictive model. Lastly, I investigate how to reliably draw causal conclusions from observational data in global health.
